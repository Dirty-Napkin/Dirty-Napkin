Creativity can strike at any moment and the best ideas are scribbled on a dirty napkin.

When you share your story

with another person, you are inviting them into your world, and in doing so, you build a larger community of people with shared worlds. That perspective is what creates connection– with individuals and with brands.

When we first started Dirty Napkin in 2023, we hardly knew the difference between a font and a color. We genuinely thought that Comic Sans and blue were the same thing. Despite inexperience, we were eager and wanted to expand our design skills to the real world. Fast forward to modern day and we are a well-oiled machine.

At its core, Dirty Napkin is a two-pronged entity:

If you are currently pursuing your passion, we can design awesome graphics for you that boldly communicate who you are and draw in like-minded people.

If you haven’t yet started, let us be the inspiration. If a small team of 4 can create something that alters your world, you can do it too.

If you are an independent creator, a heartfelt business, or our childhood crushes who are finally into us now that we’ve made it big and successful in the industry, we want to hear from you.
