approach

The three of us met in college and quickly bonded over an ambition for our design work. We started Dirty Napkin out of an eagerness to do work for clients we cared about.

We approach each of our design projects with that same eagerness and a desire to build unique worlds that understand and elevate our clients’ missions. This is done through custom brand strategy and relentless iteration.
