LIGHTS DOWN.

The Lemonade Stand is a theater production company that champions up-and-coming playwrights and performers in Philly. It’s essentially the same as if a child’s lemonade stand had the budget of an adult: putting in the hard work, selling your product to the community, and embracing whimsy.

DIY small-scale performances attract the right groups: artists who create uniquely original pieces and an audience who actually cares about them. These shows are for the absurd, the bizarre, the silly, and the personal– anybody in the “other” category.

We were packed with work, but AJ convinced us to take him on and we tried out a new design sprint model. We designed the logos over two weeks with meetings squeezed into lunch breaks, nights, and weekends. It was an absolute blast.

The Lemonade Stand brand is nothing if not bold, modern, and tart. The bright, vibrating colors are integral to bringing that energy to life.

At the core of The Lemonade Stand, Fresh Squeezed is a seasonal festival to showcase never-before-seen work-in-progress plays. It is an opportunity for local playwrights to share their work, and for the community to celebrate the fact that Philly is overflowing out of its butt with talent.
